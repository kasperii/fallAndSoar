Orientation is key when working with VR experiences - since we can not move the character between positions without risk of cyber sickness, and the immersion has payoff in orientation. The game genre point and click is forgiving, since they often are intentionally dissorientating.

To help players out a bit on the way, we created a map of the layout. Added to this was a icon showing both orientation and position of the camera in the game. This could be done by changing the position based on the view cameras rotation.
